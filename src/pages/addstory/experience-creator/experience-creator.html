What is an experience all about?

Storytelling is a wonderful way to share our life’s unique journey.

Experiences is a place to easily capture a memory and create a story using dates, location, people whom shared the experience, images and videos.

It can also be used as a tool to capture a significant time or place in life or an important object which you can build a story around for prosperity.

By sharing experiences, we have the opportunity to connect, understand, share and feel closer to each other’s journey.
